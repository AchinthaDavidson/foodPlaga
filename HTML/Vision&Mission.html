Vision & Mission

Almost in everyone’s life a phase comes when we have to move out from our native place for higher education or for career commitments.. When me and my friends shifted to Malabe for higher education without our support system “Our Family” it was a challenging task to find a place to order Healthy, Hygienic, Home made food which could remind us of our Mom’s Magic

The hardships of that phase encouraged us and the idea of getting into the virtual world of food delivery and catering came into shape. That day we decided to create a brand in the area of food & catering, providing the customer’s convenience & choice of ordering & enjoying “Their food Their way !! Anytime, Any day !!

To become the most preferred digital app based online restaurant we launched The Food plaga Our Mission is to become your first choice in ordering your favorite food online whenever you feel hungry. So stop starving & start ordering your favorite food at Food plaga
